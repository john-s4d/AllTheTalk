AllTheTalk.com is a website dedicated to categorizing and distributing relevant syndicated content throughout the web. This is done using two automated applications ("Bots") to find a read information available on the world wide web. The AllTheTalk Bots are designed to according to industry standards. They will check and adhere to robots.txt files and to META tags. They will also browse hosts at a comfortable to prevent overloading of any servers.

Webmasters: If you believe the there is a problem with the methods the Bots use to browse and retrieve information from your site, please send an email to info@allthetalk.com with your concerns.

The AllTheTalk Crawler searches the world wide web for pages that contain informative syndicated content, specifically RSS or Atom Feeds.
There are two methods to prevent The AllTheTalk Crawler from following links on your pages to other pages or documents:

1.Place the following meta tag in the head of your HTML document:
<META NAME="AllTheTalk Crawler" CONTENT="nofollow">

2. Add the following entry to your robots.txt file:
user-agent: AllTheTalk Crawler
disallow: /

To learn more about meta tags and the robot.txt file, please refer to http://www.robotstxt.org.

If you have any questions or concerns, please contact via email: crawler_bot@allthetalk.com or info@allthetalk.com

The AllTheTalk Feeder retrieives syndicated content from RSS or Atom Feeds.

If you wish to prevent The AllTheTalk Feeder from retrieving content from any feeds on your site:

Add the following entry to your robots.txt file:
user-agent: AllTheTalk Feeder
disallow: /

If you wish to prevent the AllTheTalkFeeder from retrieving content from a specific feed on your site:

Add the following entry to your robots.txt file:

user-agent: AllTheTalk Feeder
disallow: /feedname.xml

where feedname.xml is the file that contains the feed data.

To learn more about meta tags and the robot.txt file, please refer to http://www.robotstxt.org.

If you have any questions or concerns, please contact via email: feeder_bot@allthetalk.com or info@allthetalk.com
